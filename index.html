Our Mission

We connect veterinary equipment donors with clinics and shelters worldwide. By redistributing vital tools and resources, we help improve animal health and welfare in underserved communities across the globe.

How You Can Help

We accept gently used veterinary equipment, supplies, and monetary contributions to support animal-health organizations in need.
